My Development Workflow

  • Step 1: Product design Research

    First, I take time to understand the client’s business, goals, and target audience. I research similar products and websites to get a clear idea of what design and features will work best for their needs.

  • Step 2: Development

    Once the design’s ready, I start building. I set up the structure, write clean code, and make sure everything works across devices. I focus on turning the design into a fast, functional website.

  • Step 3: Review & Launch

    After building, I test everything — layout, responsiveness, speed. I fix any bugs, make small improvements, then send it to the client for feedback. Once it’s all good, I launch.
